üéØ Course Objective

To enhance the skills to effectively shape organizational behaviour, implement successful change strategies, foster innovation, manage digital transitions, and build inclusive, adaptable, and sustainable work environments.

MODULE 1: Organizational Culture Dynamics (8 Hours)

Understanding organizational culture, elements and levels, leadership‚Äôs role, assessing culture, climate vs. culture, subcultures, culture as a competitive advantage, types (Clan, Adhocracy, Market, Hierarchy), impact of digitalization, theoretical frameworks (Schein, Deal & Kennedy), purpose-driven environments, collaboration, and teamwork.

MODULE 2: Change Management Models (10 Hours)

Overview of change management, organizational change process, challenges, drivers, incremental vs. transformational change, leadership challenges, change models (Lewin, Kotter, McKinsey 7S, ADKAR, Bridges), Competing Values Framework, and leadership strategies for successful implementation.

MODULE 3: Building Digital Culture (10 Hours)

Digital transformation and cultural impact, technology and AI, challenges and opportunities, strategies for digital culture, cultural barriers, stakeholder engagement, collaboration, cultural audits, gap analysis, mapping, and benchmarking.

MODULE 4: Agile Change Management (8 Hours)

Adapting to digital era change, KPIs for change, challenges and success factors, leveraging technology, addressing resistance, ethical and digital transformation, agile frameworks, crisis management, institutionalizing change.

MODULE 5: Trends and Practices (9 Hours)

Diversity, Equity, Inclusion, and Belongingness (DEIB), hybrid work models, purpose-driven and data-driven culture, innovation-driven culture, cross-cultural sensitivity, sustainability, automation, CSR, and change management.

üéì Course Outcomes (CO)

  1. Analyze the impact of organizational culture on behavior, decision-making, and digital transformation using cultural frameworks.
  2. Interpret change management models and strategies with leadership focus for successful implementation.
  3. Assess technology‚Äôs role in digital culture and design strategies for overcoming cultural barriers.
  4. Compare agile change management approaches addressing digital and ethical transformation challenges.
  5. Demonstrate awareness of DEIB, hybrid work, and data-driven cultures for effective change management.
